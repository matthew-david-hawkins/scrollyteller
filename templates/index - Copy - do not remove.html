Politician Twitter Comparison

Please select two politicians from the dropdown to compare against each other.


This is the start of section 1. In this chart we show the twitter popularity of two politicians as layers on a leaflet map with blue and orange markers.

For this dataset we used the twitter API to pull tweets related to each politician based on a specific hashtag.

Then we used the location of each user who tweeted about the politician and mapped the location on this chart.

We only included tweets that originated in the United States.


This is the end of section 1




This is the start of section 2. Here we show a multi-trace line plot tracking user interactivity with each politician..

To track interactivity we took the aggregate of the favorites+retweets for each day and mapped it on this chart.

Hence this shows how much activity each politician is getting each day by calculating the sum of favorites + retweets.

For this dataset, we are taking the politicians 3200 most recent tweets. However the dataset will grow over time as more data is processed into the database.


This is the end of section 2




This is the beginning of section 3. Here we show the 5 most common words in the politician's tweets, as well as their most popular tweet ever.

The most popular tweet is the sum of favorites and retweets for a single tweet.

To get the 5 most common words we extracted all of the politician's tweets and counted the frequency at which each word appeared.

We removed the 500 most common words in the English language to create a population of unique words.


This is the end of section 3
